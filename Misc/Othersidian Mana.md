Othersidian Mana comes in a variety of different colors, indicating different affinities in different types of magic.

Not to be confused with [[Timeline]]s and their variants, Othersidian Mana is the power source of all Timeline abilities. Hailing from [[The Otherside]], Othersidian Mana is a potent, extremely volatile liquid substance that flows beneath the fabric of reality. Used to fuel [[Timeline Gems]], feed [[Petracytes]], and sustain [[Othersidian Entity|Othersidian Entities]].
