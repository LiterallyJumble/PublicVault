# **History**

#### **Early Days**
Ahn7 was created for one purpose: Create. Given life by [[Primus]], the nine, small, primitive [[AHN model|AHN models]] were created out of wood and life essence and left on a remote planet devoid of sapient life, beginning to build up their new homes. Through communication with her sisters, Ahn7 was convinced to create a civilization of machines. Ahn7 brought up the idea of staying isolated on their beginning island to keep the native life of the planet safe from their rapid industrialization, protecting the fragile life of their planet and the balance of the atmosphere as well as she could, introducing safeguards and emission control. Ahn7 was very caring for all things, more than any of her sisters (not to say they weren't caring, she just cared the deepest) and eventually decided her section of the island would be a haven for those who decide to live in her town, setting laws and rules to benefit everyone while making sure work still got done.

After many years the machines around her began to properly industrialize, replacing their softer, primitive bodies with metal, and revolutionizing their society, sparking a massive increase in development.

#### **Mid History**
After around 500 years, the towns began to form into cities, Ahn7's [[City 7]] made sure to keep the natural feel of a small town with restrictions on the heights of buildings, number of people living within, and designated protected parks and wildlife areas. 

Ahn7 began to wonder if being the only robot capable of designing and building other models was wrong, and began working on a robot with the potential to create other bots itself. While her sisters thought this was a bad idea, [[Xeno Carter (MACHINE)]] was soon built with all the knowledge needed to create [[Pseudo-Organic Machine|Pseudo-Organic Machines]]. Understanding the dangers of an independent creator, Ahn7 stopped with [[Xeno Carter (MACHINE)|Mr Carter]] and passed a law that knowledge of creation could not be shared for safety reasons.

It was around this time that Ahn7 was approached by [[Ahn3]] with a gift, an upgrade module capable of accessing the mind of any bot she wanted a [[HAX Chip]] . Ahn7 believed this was a gross violation of the rights of her people, but was reassured that was not the purpose. [[Ahn3]] informed her it was to be given to someone she trusts, loves, or otherwise knows won't abuse it, for its true purpose was to be used on her. All of the [[AHN model|AHN models]] had a mind control kink, wishing to be taken over by someone else, but Ahn7 knew the dangers and never ended up using it. This, however, proved to be even more dangerous than simply giving it to someone she trusted. Months after being put into storage, an unknown person broke in and stole it, leading to multiple crimes being committed under the noses of all involved. The chip wasn't found until [[Jack9]] woke up years later mentioning a strange screen in his vision appearing next to the heads of those he looked at.
