# **History**

Skrap is a living file, specifically one documenting [[Bubble Timeline]]. Although he was created by files on Bubble, he hates being compared to him and insists he is his own separate being, despising the shadow of the man he lives in.

Skrap is not alone however, [[Skrap!Avacon]] and [[Skrap!Onyx]] living within [[Core Alpha]]'s matrix.
