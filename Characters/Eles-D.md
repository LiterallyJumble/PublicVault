# **History**

#### **Early Days**

Created in [[The Bio-Wing]], Eles-D Was created as an experiment on genetically modifying a [[Mimic Slime]] to see how editing its genome could effect the resulting specimen. Xeno used parts of the remains of two Mimic Slimes found to have passed away from unknown causes, believed to have been lovers. After trial and error and almost needing to terminate the project, Eles-D finally stabilized enough for the project to continue. A small creature without a shape of its own being given behavior-adjusting compounds and chemicals to keep it from acting out. LSD was found to be the most effective compound at keeping Eles stable, and her body eventually began producing it on its own, finally stabilizing her mind but not her body.

Eles continued to be experimented on, and while she did retain some feelings and fuzzy memories of her biological parents, her artificial nature caused her to lack the parental personality and knowledge most Mimic Slimes are formed with, leaving her as a blank slate.

Unfortunately, due to reasons outside of Xeno's control, testing was halted halfway, leaving Eles without the capability to express her own feelings or desires, going so deep that she doesn't even know them herself.
