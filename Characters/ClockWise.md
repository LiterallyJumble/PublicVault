# **History**

#### **Early Days**
Clockwise was the first of [[Mother]]'s creations with self-awareness, born in the scrapyard beneath [[Xeno Laboratories]]. She quickly took a liking to creating and designing new robots alongside [[Mother]] in the underground scrapyard.

#### **Mid History**
Over the years, Clockwise eventually left the scrapyard to find herself, exploring her identity and independence.

#### **Later Years**
Clockwise accepted the fact that she was trans and commissioned [[Xeno Carter]] to build her a new body reflecting her true identity.
