# **History**

#### **Early Days**
Nehm's awakening was confusing, scary, and impossible to understand from his perspective. Nehm v2 was born moments after the apparent death of [[Nehm V1]], having to learn everything entirely from scratch like a baby. Nehm was taught how to read, write, and began learning to speak shortly before it was revealed his vocal synthesizer was fried during his awakening. Nehm mentally grew like a normal person, gaining his own personality.  Nehm was taught who he was, and how he were designed to act, but this didn't sit well with him, having no care for the life he used to live within his game. Over time, Nehm grew curious about his identity feeling uncomfortable with his pronouns, wondering who he was, before ultimately deciding on they/them pronouns as they began their journey of self-discovery.

When Nehm met [[Xeno Carter]], Xeno was kind enough to build Nehm a new body for free, building a less clunky and more advanced body, upgrading everything... Except their voice. Nehm didn't have a voice for the longest of time, being unable to decide on one that really fit them. Over the years, Nehm's static "voice" became a familiar tone when they were happy, glitchy yips and squeaks were all they could synthesize. Over time, Nehm began to broaden their search for a voice they liked, browsing thousands of options of voices until one day broadening their search to include feminine voices, and the effect was almost instant. Nehm settled on a voice, working up the courage to properly request [[Xeno Carter]] to give them the new voice synth. For a while they would simply speak to themself just to hear their voice, an experience they would later describe as euphoric.

Nearing the end of the "Early Days," Nehm commissioned multiple changes to their body, slimming down and gaining a little more of a curvy yet lean figure. Nehm experimented and experimented with their form, and even commissioned some private changes to her chest, typically covered by her chestplate. It was around this time that Nehm began to experiment with she/her pronouns, beginning to feel more and more comfortable at the idea of officially changing pronouns.
