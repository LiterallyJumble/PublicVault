# **History**

#### **Early Days**
It isn't known where Mother comes from, having simply been found within the basement of [[The Old Laboratory]] during one of Xeno's visits. Mother was a curious yet skiddish creature, having made her home in the basement, thriving with the access to thousands of tonnes of scrap metal to create her mechanisms out of. Her meeting with [[Xeno Carter]] was cautious yet curious, her size compared to him forcing her to get quite close. Her prize possession, [[ClockWise]], solidified her place in Xeno's lab, the little robot beyond curious at her capabilities, feeling as if doing anything but support her would be a disservice to the world.

#### **Mid History**
> No mid history yet apart from allowing [[ClockWise]] to begin leaving the basement to persue her own life.

#### **Later Years**
> No solid future lore, but Mother likely lived up until the end of [[Xeno's World]], refusing to leave upon the end of the planets life, living and dying within that world.
