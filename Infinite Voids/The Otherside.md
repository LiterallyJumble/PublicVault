## **History and Significance**

The Otherside is a mysterious world unreachable through any known methods, nothing is known about this mysterious space apart from two facts:

1. The Otherside is a world akin to [[The Void]], an endless expanse that follows no known universal shape, being truly infinite

2. All Timeline magic comes from The Otherside, the source of all pure and potent Timeline Energy.
